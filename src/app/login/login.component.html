The Award is one of its kind and focuses on Food Safety Maturity Assessment to promote excellence. Since its inception in 2010, the CII Food Safety Award has become a symbol of credibility and pride for Food Business operators. More than recognition, the Awards measure performance and provide detailed feedback from an external perspective on opportunities to excel.
